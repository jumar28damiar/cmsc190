The Monitoring and Detection of Ecosystems Changes for Enhancing Resilience and Adaptation in the Philippines (MODECERA) is a DOST and DOST-PCAARRD funded program which aims to set up long-term monitoring system in eight selected ecosystems to enhance resilience and adaptation of agriculture and natural resources sector through promotion of science and technology based management and policy decisions.
